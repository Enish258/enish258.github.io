Publications

Cyber Attack Detection in SCADA in Smart Grid using Autoencoder and Incremental Learning

International Journal of Novel Research and Development(IJNRD)Volume 7, Issue 12,December 2022

The paper explores using Machine Learning models, specifically Autoencoder and Incremental Learning, to predict SCADA attacks. It emphasizes the need for improved models to handle dynamic data and mentions the potential of unsupervised learning algorithms in the future. The Autoencoder is highlighted as an efficient solution for training in the absence of abundant labeled data.
